About
the
bakery

A space you want to stay in

Our bakery is located in a cozy loft space with high ceilings, natural light, and a warm atmosphere.

This is not just a place to grab coffee on the go — it's a place where you want to linger. We've thoughtfully designed an area with comfortable tables and power outlets so freelancers, students, and anyone who enjoys peaceful morning rituals can work, read, and relax in comfort.

Our philosophy is honesty, warmth, and openness.

We bake with attention to detail and a love for simple ingredients: only flour, water,
sourdough, spices — and a little bit of magic. We have a friendly team, a cozy atmosphere,
and a place you'll want to come back to.
